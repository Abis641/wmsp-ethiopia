About Us

Welcome to Koshe Waste Solutions, where our commitment to sustainability and community well-being drives everything we do. Located in the heart of Koshe, Ethiopia, we are dedicated to transforming waste management practices and fostering a cleaner, healthier environment for all.

Our Mission

At Koshe Waste Solutions, our mission is to implement innovative and sustainable waste solutions that not only address current waste challenges but also empower our community. We strive to reduce waste, promote recycling, and ensure safe and efficient disposal methods, creating a lasting positive impact on both the environment and public health.
